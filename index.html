Tea is a high-level language and runtime system
for automatically selecting and executing valid statistical tests.

Tea aims to lower the barrier for conducting valid, reporducible statistical analyses for novices and experts alike. Tea achieves this by eliciting users' domain knowledge and hypotheses and verifying properties of the data to infer applicable, valid statistical tests.
